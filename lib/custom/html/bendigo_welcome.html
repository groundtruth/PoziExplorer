COMMUNITY COMPASS


Community Compass is a mapping system that displays details on your property, as well as nearby Council facilities and services, by simply typing in your address.

If you are having difficulty or need support accessing the information on this mapping system, please contact the City of Greater Bendigo by phone on 5434 6000 or email.

Please note: Information in this mapping system has been derived from various sources and while all care has been taken in its presentation, it may not have been prepared for, or be suitable for, legal, engineering or surveying purposes. The City of Greater Bendigo cannot accept any responsibility for errors, omissions, or positional inaccuracies, although notification of any such errors is appreciated. Email your feedback.
