Getting Started

SEARCH



Enter 3 characters or more to search for addresses, properties, towns ...

TABS



Select a property (search for it or click on it), then click on any tab on the right to access more detail about this property.

LAYERS



Click on the left bar or on the list symbol to expand the list of available layers.



Tick a check box to view the corresponding layer on the map. Untick it to hide the layer from the map.
